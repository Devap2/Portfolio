About Me.

Hi there! My name is Alex and I am a student at the University of Rotterdam where I am studying to become a software engineer.

In my free time, I enjoy solving problems, exploring new things, and learning new skills. I have 3+ years of work experience, and I love to take on new projects and challenges. Some of the projects that I have worked on in the past include Studian and Byble, which you can check out in the "projects" section.

I am always looking for new opportunities to grow and learn, and I am excited to see what the future holds for me in the field of software engineering.

When i'm not in front of a computer screen, i'm probably drawing, playing the piano, playing soccer or hanging out with friends.
